The Gist

Greyling Grove is a place for spirits that are yet to be placed to rest, one of many places between life and the afterlife. However, this does not mean that they skip on celebrating holidays!

Yet there's one small problem, thanks to the whole 'basically being dead' thing, the vast majority of the people there simply don't have the willpower to deliver Christmas gifts themselves.

This is where you, the player, come into the picture.

You've been tasked with delivering presents for the people who are too lazy to do it themselves. Armed with your trusty gloves and a stamina to challenge a Bar-Headed Goose, you will be the one to deliver the presents.
