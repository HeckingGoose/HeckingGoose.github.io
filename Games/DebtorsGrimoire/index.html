What is The Debtor's Grimoire?

-An image of the player putting themselves in debt.

-An image of the player worrying about their debt.

The Debtor's Grimoire is a game about running a store where you sell spells to try and pay off your crippling debt..

-An image of the player creating a spell.

-An image of the player picking the spells for the day.

The game was made as part of the 2024 Three Thing Game jam, which can be found here.

The three 'things' that this game was designed around were:

  • Spell,
  • Banker,
  • Cripping Debt.

The game probably has plenty of bugs that I haven't encountered yet (or haven't had time to fix), as the jam took place across the course of three days and there was only one of me working on the game.
