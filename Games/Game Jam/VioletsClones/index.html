What's going on?!?!

Today Violet's returned back from work to discover a near-perfect clone of herself standing outside the back of her house.

On top of that, the clone was yelling nothing but obscenities.

Why is this happening?

Violet's pet hamster - Giuseppe - has been experimenting, and was finally successful in his goal.
To clone a human.

What now? Is humanity doomed?!?!

It may be doomed soon, but it isn't doomed yet!

Violet must beat the clone at its own game, by out-insulting it. If successful she might just be able to prevent an apocalypse of Violet clones.

This game was made for the 2025 Three Thing Game November Game Jam.

The 'things' for this game were:

  • Hamster,
  • Insulting,
  • Clones.
